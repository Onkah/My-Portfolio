ABOUT US

HUMBLE-UNI DRIVING ACADEMY


Humble-Uni Driving Academy is a driving school based in
Cosmo City, Johannesburg North in Gauteng.
It was founded in 2018. It is a registered and accredited
K53 driving school with highly competent driving instructors.

It is an institution that provides formal instruction and training
to individuals who are learning how to drive a motor vehicle.
It offers a structured curriculum to help new drivers gain the
knowledge, skills, and confidence necessary to operate a
vehicle safely and responsibly.
